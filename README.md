Smart Hauling Mod
The Smart Hauling mod eliminates the most tedious hauling tasks in RimWorld by increasing your colonists' carry capacity and optimizing their hauling logic. This mod ensures that instead of hauling single units, colonists gather multiple items in one trip based on their configured inventory limits.

Smart Hauling Features

Enhanced Carry Capacity: Customize your colonists' carry capacity in the mod settings, with values ranging from 1 to 200.

Smart Gathering: Colonists will recognize and pick up items of the same type along their path until their inventory capacity is full, making every hauling trip more efficient.

Simple Integration: Designed to work alongside existing game systems with minimal setup.

Compatibility Notes

Warning: Do not run your save without reading this first, or you might lose all your hard work. Always back up your save file before adding major hauling mods.

It is recommended to place this mod above other hauling/stacking mods in your load order.

The mod was created using AI due to the lack of similar working alternatives.
